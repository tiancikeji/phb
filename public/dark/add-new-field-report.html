Explain the report in a few words.
Introduction: Describe what you are doing, when, where, and other information as suitable.
Methodology: Describe how you are carrying out the experiment, what instruments you are using, how you defined the measuring parameters etc.
Result: Show data (as table, graph or 3D surface, whatever is appropriate); describe the results but do not interpret them!
Interpretation and Analyses: Here you show how you use the data to define the target of measurement. If specific tasks are given of how to use the data to define the object of interest, here is the place to show your work.
Summary and Conclusion: In brief words, summarize the findings of your experiment. This should not be a simple repetition of things listed in (01) - (04) or the abstract.
References: If you used resources from other people's work (scientific papers, manuals, internet information, etc.) list them here.
